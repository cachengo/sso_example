Welcome {{ user.firstname }} to the Mobile CORD (M-CORD) on Arm demonstration, brought to you by Orange(R) and Cachengo(TM). This portal demonstration consists of several micro services representing the VNFs required initialize the fabric that allows the various nodes in front of you to communicate with one another. The authentication mechanism is implemented as a single sign-on (SSO) with two-factor authentication, utilizing the IMEI or MAC of your device, along with your face to establish your identity. While the VNFs are hosted on Arm Cortex processors, the neural networking and policy handling is processed on Arm Mali GPUs. This 42-node reference design is operating on less than 200 Watts of power.
